    Remember the big ideas
    • Choose tests based on your research question, variable types, and study design (independent vs paired, number of groups).
    • Check assumptions (e.g. normality, equal variances, independent observations).
    • If multiple tests are appropriate, start with the most powerful test whose assumptions you can justify.
